Risks & Mitigations

We know the risks. We have a plan.

Honest assessment — platform dependency, competition, solo execution, and growth.

Platform dependency
Stripe or Cloudflare changes terms. Mitigation: Stripe Connect is industry standard with no real alternative risk. Cloudflare is edge-agnostic — can migrate to Vercel or AWS Workers if needed with minimal code changes.
Big players add a free tier
If Shopify launches a "test one product" free mode, differentiation narrows. Mitigation: They're architected for full stores. Serving validation-stage sellers requires a fundamentally different UX and pricing philosophy — not just a toggle.
Solo founder · execution
Largely mitigated. Full product already built and deployed. Under $100/yr infrastructure. No burn rate. Profitable at first paying customer. Risk surface is unusually small for this stage.
User acquisition
Pre-marketing, pre-revenue. Planned channels: every store carries an instxnt.xyz backlink (organic loop), creator partnerships, alpha waitlist at instxnt.xyz. Growth is the active focus after product stabilization.
